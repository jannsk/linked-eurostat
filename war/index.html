Eurostat Linked Data Wrapper

Welcome to the EXPERIMENTAL Eurostat wrapper to Linked Data. The data is open and free (Eurostat license policy).

We provide a table of contents (also available: table of contents as RDF) and a list of recently updated datasets.

Nearby: about the Eurostat wrapper.
